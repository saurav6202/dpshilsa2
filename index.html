About Our School

D.P.S Public School was established in 1995 with a vision to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with holistic development. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most prestigious educational institutions in the region.

Our campus spans over 10 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including smart classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a spacious library, and extensive sports facilities. We believe in creating an environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking.

With a team of highly qualified and dedicated teachers, we strive to provide personalized attention to each student, helping them discover their potential and develop into confident, responsible global citizens.
